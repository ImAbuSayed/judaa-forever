[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Tere akhiyan da nasha mainu lagge dark and deep,
Saari raat sochda haan tainu main losing my sleep,
Mud mud vekhda haan mukhda tera losing control,
Sohniye teriyan adavan ne chhu leya my soul.

[Hook 2]
Naina naal naina di sadi crazy conversation,
Roz labhda haan tere vich my motivation,
Raatan nu turda haan kalla main fighting the tension,
Ban gayi ae tu meri sohni sweet obsession.

[Verse 1]
Kaaliyan raatan ch kalla karda haan wait,
Seene di dhadkan meri mangdi ae romantic date,
Roz hanjuan de paani ch drowning in vain,
Kalla main bhijjda haan ajj vi in the cold rain.
Palkan te rakhiyaan ne teriyan hidden nishaniyan,
Beh ke main karda haan yaad those tragic kahaniyan,
Ajj main chhadd da haan koshish to leave it behind,
Yaadan ch mud mud aundi ae tu blowing my mind.

[Pre-Chorus]
Kalla tarian di chhaon vich single and broken,
Bulliyan te ajj vi ne words unspoken,
Agg wangu jalda haan main burning so bright,
Bacha ke main rakheya ae love token tight.

[Chorus]
Sohniye judaa forever tu kar gayi ae mainu,
Puchda haan jaake main shadows ch tainu,
Dull jande akhiyan de paani ch silent tears fall,
Raatan nu darda haan kalla hun missing your call.
Judaa forever main reh gaya haan kalla,
Tere ishq vich hoya haan crazy te jhalla,
Mangda haan rab ton main only your face,
Dil diyan saariyan umeedan ne lost in the space.

[Verse 2]
Kalli motor di awaz utte riding so dark,
Gaddi de sheeshe thalle karke leaving a mark,
Agge nu bhajjda haan kalla main burning the floor,
Ajj vi main tere piche turda haan wanting some more.
Saari raat kalla main ghumda haan quiet roads te,
Sohne yaaran naal behnda haan on the long routes te,
Karda haan wafa par tu nikli ae fake,
Seene vich dil mera tuttya for your own sake.

[Verse 3]
Sohniye hun main na auna kade back to your side,
Ajj ton main chhaddta ae rona swallowing my pride,
Piche nu takkda nai mud ke the ashes I leave,
Zindagi ch sikheya haan kalla now how to achieve.
Kaale dhuyan de badlaan ch masking my pain,
Kalla main jittda haan duniya through the cold rain,
Ajj kal chalda haan seena kadd feeling so strong,
Dhokhe main tere wangu nayi karda keeping it wrong.

[Bridge]
Nede tu aaja soniye feeling the heat,
Aakhda Saeed tainu my heartbeat,
Chhu ke taan vekh le this crazy desire,
Tere naina di kashish lighting the fire,
Takda haan tainu with passion so deep,
Ban ja tu meri a promise to keep.

[Final Chorus]
Sohniye judaa forever tu kar gayi ae mainu,
Puchda haan jaake main shadows ch tainu,

Tere ishq vich hoya haan crazy te jhalla,

Zindagi ch turda haan agge leaving this space.
