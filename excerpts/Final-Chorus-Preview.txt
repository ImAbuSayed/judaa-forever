Sohniye judaa forever tu kar gayi ae mainu,
Puchda haan jaake main shadows ch tainu,
Dull jande akhiyan de paani ch silent tears fall,
Raatan nu darda haan kalla hun missing your call.
Judaa forever main reh gaya haan kalla,
Tere ishq vich hoya haan crazy te jhalla,
Seene cho mukk gayi ae aas now finding my grace,
Zindagi ch turda haan agge leaving this space.
