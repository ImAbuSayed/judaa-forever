Kaaliyan raatan ch kalla karda haan wait,
Seene di dhadkan meri mangdi ae romantic date,
Roz hanjuan de paani ch drowning in vain,
Kalla main bhijjda haan ajj vi in the cold rain.
Palkan te rakhiyaan ne teriyan hidden nishaniyan,
Beh ke main karda haan yaad those tragic kahaniyan,
Ajj main chhadd da haan koshish to leave it behind,
Yaadan ch mud mud aundi ae tu blowing my mind.
