Sohniye hun main na auna kade back to your side,
Ajj ton main chhaddta ae rona swallowing my pride,
Piche nu takkda nai mud ke the ashes I leave,
Zindagi ch sikheya haan kalla now how to achieve.
Kaale dhuyan de badlaan ch masking my pain,
Kalla main jittda haan duniya through the cold rain,
Ajj kal chalda haan seena kadd feeling so strong,
Dhokhe main tere wangu nayi karda keeping it wrong.
